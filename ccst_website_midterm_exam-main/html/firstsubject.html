ACT

associate of computer technology

A two-year ladderized diploma program
focused on providing foundational IT skills,
typically including computer programming,
networking, troubleshooting, and database management.
It prepares students for entry-level roles
such as technical support, web
developers, or programmers, and offers a direct pathway
into Bachelor’s degrees like BSIT or BSCS
