is the stage of a developer's journey
where they transition from simply understanding
syntax to building functional, well-structured

BSOA
Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICE PROCEDURES AND MANAGEMENT

involves organizing and managing office operations,
records management,
office technology, and supervision.

BUSINESS COMMUNICATION AND KEYBOARDING

covers advanced keyboarding and document processing,
business report writing, and shorthand machine,
shorthand techniques for recording
and transcribing meetings and dictations.

SPECIALIZED TRANSCRIPTION & INFORMATION SYSTEMS

This area specializes in technical transcription for specific fields,
commonly including Legal Transcription,
Medical Transcription, and Corporate Transcription.

BSE
Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

BUSINESS PLAN PREPARATION AND IMPLEMENTATION

is considered the cornerstone of the
BS Entrepreneurship program, often spanning multiple semesters
(e.g., Business Plan Implementation I & II).

ENTREPRENEURIAL MARKETING

shifts from traditional marketing to focus on
strategies suitable for new
ventures with limited resources.

ENTREPRENEURIAL FINANCE AND ACCOUNTING

focuses on the financial management
necessary to start, operate, and grow a new business.

HM
Hospitality Management

FOOD & BEVERAGE MANAGEMENT

focuses on food preparation, restaurant service,
menu planning, food safety, and banquet operations.
It covers both the back-of-house and
front-of-house aspects of dining.

HOTEL OPERATIONS AND ROOM DIVISION

covers the management of the lodging aspect of the industry.
It includes guest relations, front desk procedures,
housekeeping management, and maintenance.

EVENT MANAGEMENT AND CULINARY ARTS

train students to plan and execute meetings,
and social events, along with
mastering food preparation techniques
